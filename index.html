About the Project

Learning Beyond Boundaries is an Erasmus+ KA210 SCH project that aims to enhance artificial intelligence literacy and digital competencies in education. The project brings together partner schools from Germany, Turkey, and Romania to explore innovative and inclusive teaching approaches supported by AI-based tools.
