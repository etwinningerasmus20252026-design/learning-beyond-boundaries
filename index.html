About the Project

With the approved funding from the National Agency, our Erasmus+ project will run for a total duration of 20 months, starting in November 2025 and continuing until the end of June 2027. Throughout this period, three mobilities will take place. For each mobility, one of the three partner schools will host a small group of students and teachers from the other partner institutions. Before, during, and after these visits, students will collaborate online through eTwinning projects.

Project Objectives

The main objective of the project is to expand the use of artificial intelligence in everyday school life in order to reduce teachers’ workload while simultaneously supporting and personalising students’ individual learning processes. Within the framework of the three mobilities, participants will explore both the opportunities and potential risks of using AI.

Activities

The project enhances classroom practice by integrating meaningful AI-based activities into everyday lessons. During the mobility in Heidelberg (Germany), visitors will explore the city through a scavenger hunt, learn how to prepare “Käsespätzle,” a traditional German dish, and participate in a design workshop. Across all three mobilities, students will collaboratively develop a handbook on the responsible and effective use of AI.
